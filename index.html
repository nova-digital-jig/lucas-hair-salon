Our Story

Where Beauty Meets
Craftsmanship

Lucas Hair Salon has been a cornerstone of the Woodbridge community for over 15 years. What started as one stylist's passion for making people feel confident has grown into a full-service destination for hair, nails, braids, and everything in between.

Our team of experienced stylists stays ahead of the latest trends and techniques, while never losing sight of what matters most — listening to you and delivering exactly the look you envision.

Walk-ins are always welcome, and we're proud to be open seven days a week because looking your best shouldn't have to wait.
